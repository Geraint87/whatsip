Start a round and let the drinks roll in

Ever dreamt of being a Barista? Once you press Start that dream has come true! Everyone in the group will get a notification to place their order. When they've made their choice, you'll see a summary of the orders and then it's time to make the drinks!

Our fun, virtual credit based system keeps the rounds fair and easy to manage. Each drink order takes up one credit, and when you're out of credits, you can't take part in the round. Until of course, you collect some credits back by doing a round yourself!

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Whatsip? Whatsip is a mobile app that helps groups organise drink rounds and virtual tea breaks, making it easy to connect whether you’re in the office or working remotely.
How do I start a round? Simply create a group, add your members, and tap 'Start a round'. Everyone gets a notification to place their drink order, and the organiser receives a summary of all selections.
Can I use Whatsip for remote teams? Yes! Whatsip lets you start a virtual tea break, sending a video call invite to your group so you can catch up over a drink, wherever you are.
What is the credits system? Credits help keep rounds fair. Each time you join a round, you use a credit. When you do a round for others, you earn credits back!
Is Whatsip free? Whatsip is free for small groups. We offer premium plans for larger teams and extra features—see our pricing section for details.
How do I update my group or drink preferences? You can update your group details or drink preferences anytime from the app settings. Just tap on your group or profile to make changes.
